BEER GEAR

A monthly box of limited edition gear created exclusively for Mighty Beer members. Enhance your drinking experience and show your love for craft beer with awesome apparel, glassware, barware, and more that you won't find anywhere else. Icon ideas: brown box with mighty beer logo.

BEER GATHERINGS

Exclusive invitations to private events, tastings, pairings, tours and discussions at breweries, pubs, restaurants and other cool venues. Meet the makers, get the inside scoop, and hang out with like minded enthusiasts. Icon idea: invitation or ticket with mighty beer logo on it.

MIGHTY BEER MONTHLY

An old school (but super cool) printed newsletter mailed right to you, featuring interviews, reviews, brewer profiles and fun stories about beer. Full color, great writing, perfect reading over a pint or two. Icon idea: newsletter with logo

BEERS2PEERS

Members only trading network for sharing beers and forging friendships. Try beers you can't get at home through trustworthy, reliable, enthusiastic and friendly exchanges.
